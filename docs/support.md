# Support And Safety Boundary

MindCanary is a private rhythms journal. It does not diagnose, predict clinical
episodes, provide treatment, monitor safety, contact caregivers, or replace
professional care.

## Non-Sensitive Support

Use GitHub Issues for reproducible product problems that can be described
without private records. Helpful information includes:

- app version and Linux distribution;
- whether the issue involves desktop, daemon, browser extension, or package;
- concise reproduction steps; and
- copied error codes or the reviewed support-information preview.

Do not attach exports, databases, recovery secrets, browser-extension storage,
OS-keyring material, or screenshots containing private values.

## Security Reports

Use GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting as described in [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md).

## Product-Safety Feedback

Reports that the app increased rumination, encouraged excessive checking, felt
clinical or judgmental, or made connector behavior unclear are valid product
safety reports. They should not require disclosure of the user's underlying
records.

Support is best-effort during the early alpha.
